Moon Power

Controls

Up Arrow Key: Walks Up

Down Arrow Key: Walks Down

SpaceBar: Fires Moon Tiara

How To Play

The goal of the game is to keep killing until you've

defeated Queen Beryl three times. The male minions will

be killed with one attack, while the female minions

take two attacks. The Queen takes six attacks to defeat her.

You can only shoot 5 Moon Tiara attacks at once.

If three minions and boss get past you, the game is over.

Good Luck!
